About us

Welcome to MS Picasso!

Here at Ms Picasso, our goal is to show aspiring artists that they don't need fancy expensive tools to make amazing art.

To do this, we're building a community of artists who express their talents through free computer programs such as MS Paint and it's equivalents on other operating systems.

We would like to encourage you to encourage these artists as well by purchasing the art they put up for sale on our site on the Buy page.

Or if you're an aspiring artist and you want to post art or your own, check out the Submit Art page!

If you want to know some more info about your favorite artists, or if you've posted here and want to see your own statistics, go to the Artists page!

Art Guidlines

MS Picasso was made for artists of all ages, so we don't allow 18+ content.

We also display all images in a maximum 100 pixel by 100 pixel format, so larger images may not appear the same on our website.

For art put on the buy page, we take a 5% cut on each sale so that we have the funds to keep this site up and running.
